The Atelier FAQ

How do I book a specific artist?

Most artists book 2-4 months in advance. Use the form above to specify your preference, and we will notify you when their books open or if there is a cancellation.

Do you accept walk-ins?

Tattoos are strictly by appointment only. However, we do accept walk-ins for simple jewelry changes and quick piercing check-ups on weekdays between 1 PM and 5 PM.

What is your cancellation policy?

A non-refundable deposit is required to secure your date. We require at least 48 hours' notice to reschedule your appointment without losing your deposit.
